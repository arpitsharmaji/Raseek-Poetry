“Kya aap jaante hain ki kavita sirf shabdon ka mel nahi, balki hamari aatma ki aawaaz hai?” “Kavita ke har shabd mein ek alag ehsaas chhupa hota hai, jo dil ki gehraiyon tak jaakar humein ek naye drishti se jeevan dekhne ka mauka deta hai.” “Har kavita ek kahani kehati hai, jo hamare andar chhipe jazbaaton ko ujaagar karti hai. Kavita vyakti ke anubhav, sapne aur bhavnaon ka aaina hoti hai. Yeh shabdon ka jaadu hai jo na sirf man ko chhoo jata hai, balki jeevan ke naye arth bhi prastut karta hai!”
